Explore safely, learn confidently

Discover plants around you with BeWilder.

BeWilder is a safety-first plant discovery web app that combines plant scanning, a personal plant collection, a plant encyclopedia, seasonal map exploration, and interactive gameplay.

  5. Explore plants through maps and games

What BeWilder does

Scan plants

Scan, upload, or simulate an unknown plant result during the prototype demo.

Understand results

View possible matches with confidence scores, risk guidance, and clear safety reminders.

Build a collection

Save successful plant scans to a personal collection that works like a plant discovery log.

Browse an encyclopedia

Explore known plants, learn basic facts, and compare them with scan results.

Explore the map

View verified seasonal plant sightings on a public map for outdoor exploration.

Learn through gameplay

Use plant quizzes, levels, and leaderboards to make plant learning more engaging.

Safety reminder

BeWilder is a learning and exploration tool. Users should not eat or handle unknown plants based only on an app result.
